NextJob Program

Losing a job can be a challenging time. Our NextJob Program is designed to support you through the transition and help you take the next step forward.

NextJob program

Losing your job doesn’t mean losing your home. With NextJob, you will get access to:

Personal employment coach.

One-on-one guidance from a coach who will support you every step of the way.

Job search strategies.

Tips and techniques tailored to your skills to help you find the right opportunities faster.

Resume and cover letter support.

Learn how to create compelling, customized application materials that hiring managers want to see.

Personalized job search plan.

A step-by-step roadmap aligned with your career goals to keep your job search focused and effective.

Interview training.

Expert advice and interview practice to help you stand out and make a strong impression.

Weekly workshops.

Join sessions on key topics designed to support your journey.

Personal brand building.

Learn how to create or update your online presence to reflect your experience, skills, and goals.
