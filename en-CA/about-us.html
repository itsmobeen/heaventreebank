ABOUT US

Our Promise

Haventree: more than a name, it’s our promise to Canadians.

We’re a privately-owned Canadian bank built on one core belief: no matter the season of life, growth is possible. That’s why we invest in great people, strong partnerships, and smart tools, to make banking feel a little more human, and a lot more helpful.

Real life. Real customers. Real resilience.

Behind every Haventree customer is a story of hope, change, and becoming.

Branching out to build the bank Canadians deserve.

OSFI Regulated.

We are federally regulated by the Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions (OSFI).

CDIC Insured.

We are CDIC-insured, which means your deposits are protected by the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation (CDIC) if Haventree Bank fails.

We’re a Schedule 1 Bank.

We are a Canadian financial institution licensed under the Bank Act.
