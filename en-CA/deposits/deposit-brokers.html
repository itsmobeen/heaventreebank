BENEFITS

Guaranteed returns on investment

Offer your clients peace of mind and growth.

As a Deposit Broker, you know how important it is to provide clients with secure and reliable investment options. Our Guaranteed Investment Certificates (GICs) offer a strong combination of safety, competitive returns, and flexible terms, designed to support your client’s financial goals with confidence.

Guaranteed returns.

Our GICs offer fixed interest rates, providing your clients with predictable, reliable returns on their investments.

Capital protection.

The principal investment is fully protected, making GICs an ideal solution for risk-averse clients who want steady income without compromising security.

Competitive rates.

We offer some of the most competitive interest rates in the market, helping your clients maximize returns while maintaining peace of mind.

Flexible Terms.

Choose from a wide range of term lengths, from short- to long-term options. This flexibility allows you to align investment strategies with each client’s unique financial goals.

Reputation and Trust.

Partner with a trusted financial institution known for delivering consistent value, stability, and support to brokers and their clients.

Your clients’ deposits are protected.

Haventree Bank is a member of the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation (CDIC)*.

Our GICs are eligible for CDIC deposit insurance, subject to the maximum coverage limits outlined in the CDIC brochure “Protecting Your Deposits”.
